# Dashboard Roles & Specifications

The Expressway Management System features 15 specialized dashboards:

1. **Super Admin Dashboard**: National Command Center.
2. **Highway Admin Dashboard**: Operational Monitoring.
3. **Toll Management Dashboard**: Real-Time Toll Control.
4. **FASTag Management Dashboard**: Digital Payment Monitoring.
5. **Vehicle Monitoring Dashboard**: AI Surveillance Center.
6. **Traffic Analytics Dashboard**: Data Intelligence.
7. **Incident Management Dashboard**: Emergency Response Control.
8. **Emergency Support Dashboard**: Rapid Response Center.
9. **Road Maintenance Dashboard**: Infrastructure Operations.
10. **CCTV Monitoring Dashboard**: Smart Surveillance System.
11. **Route & Lane Monitoring Dashboard**: Traffic Operations.
12. **Challan & Fine Dashboard**: Law Enforcement.
13. **Fuel & Patrol Tracking Dashboard**: Field Operations.
14. **Reports & Analytics Dashboard**: Government Intelligence.
15. **Control Room Dashboard**: National Highway Command Center.

Each dashboard is unique in layout and purpose, following the premium government enterprise aesthetic.
