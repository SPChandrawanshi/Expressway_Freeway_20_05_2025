# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Smart Expressway & Freeway Management System

### 1. Project Overview
- **Product Name**: Smart Expressway & Freeway Management System
- **Product Type**: Government Enterprise-Level Smart Highway Management Platform
- **Vision**: To build a modern AI-powered smart expressway management ecosystem for monitoring, controlling, analyzing, and managing highways, toll systems, traffic movement, emergency services, surveillance systems, and government reporting operations.

### 2. Main Objectives
- Centralized highway control system.
- Smart toll and traffic management.
- Real-time monitoring and government-grade reporting.
- AI-based analytics and predictions.
- Emergency response optimization and public safety enhancement.

### 3. Target Users
- **Super Admin**: National/State-level control authority.
- **Highway Admin**: Highway operational management.
- **Toll Manager**: Toll plaza operations.
- **CCTV Operator**: Surveillance monitoring.
- **Emergency Team**: Emergency response handling.
- **Patrol Officer**: Highway field operations.
- **Finance Officer**: Revenue and analytics management.
- **Maintenance Team**: Road repair and maintenance.
- **Public Users**: Complaint and emergency support.

### 4. Core Modules
1. **Authentication & Security**: Secure login, RBAC, OTP, session management.
2. **Super Admin Command Center**: State-wide monitoring, national analytics, revenue intelligence.
3. **Toll Management System**: Toll booth management, lane monitoring, FASTag scanning.
4. **FASTag Management**: Verification, balance validation, recharge tracking, blacklist detection.
5. **Vehicle Monitoring System**: Real-time tracking, overspeed detection, ANPR/AI recognition.
6. **Traffic Analytics**: Density monitoring, peak hour analysis, congestion prediction.
7. **Incident & Accident Management**: Accident reporting, road blockage, emergency assignment.
8. **Emergency Support System**: SOS requests, ambulance/police tracking, dispatch center.
9. **CCTV Monitoring System**: Live feeds, health monitoring, motion detection, smart wall.
10. **Route & Lane Monitoring**: Lane management, route diversion, signal monitoring.
11. **Challan & Fine Management**: Overspeed challans, toll violation fines, payment tracking.
12. **Fuel & Patrol Tracking**: GPS tracking, fuel consumption, driver logs.
13. **Road Maintenance System**: Damage reporting, work orders, repair tracking.
14. **Reports & Analytics**: Automated generation and export (PDF/Excel).
15. **AI Command Center**: Patterns detection, traffic prediction, risk analysis.
16. **Public Portal**: Complaints, traffic updates, toll calculator.

### 5. UI/UX Requirements
- **Style**: Smart City Command Center / Government Intelligence Dashboard.
- **Theme**: Modern enterprise UI, Dark premium dashboard, Glassmorphism.
- **Branding**: MP Government, GoI, and Expressway logos.
- **Rules**: Primary hover border `#6D214F`, global shadow `rgba(0,0,0,0.35) 0px 5px 15px`.
- **Responsiveness**: Optimized for Mobile, Tablet, Desktop, and Ultra-Wide screens.

### 6. Technical Requirements
- **Frontend Stack**: React.js, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Recharts, React Leaflet, AG Grid, Socket.IO.
- **Backend Stack**: Node.js, Express.js, Prisma ORM, MySQL/PostgreSQL, Socket.IO, JWT.

### 7. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- Faster toll operations and reduced congestion.
- Faster emergency response times.
- Increased revenue efficiency.
- Improved public safety and highway monitoring.
